What are Dermatological Conditions ?

Dermatological conditions are disorders that affect the skin, ranging from mild irritations to serious diseases. They include inflammatory conditions (e.g., eczema, psoriasis), infections (bacterial, viral, fungal), allergic reactions, and autoimmune disorders. Some are temporary, while others may be chronic or hereditary. Proper diagnosis and treatment depend on the type and severity of the condition.

Dermatological Classification and Conditions

Acne

Acne Is A Skin Condition That Occurs When Your Hair Follicles Become Plugged With Oil And Dead Skin Cells. It Causes Whiteheads, Blackheads Or Pimples. Acne Is Most Common Among Teenagers, Though It Affects People Of All Ages.

Actinic Keratosis

Actinic Keratosis (AK), Also Known As Solar Keratosis, Is A Precancerous Skin Condition That Arises From Prolonged Sun Exposure. It Typically Appears As Rough, Scaly Patches Or Growths On Areas Of The Skin That Are Frequently Exposed To The Sun, Such As The Face, Ears, Neck, Scalp, Chest, Backs Of The Hands, Forearms, Or Lips.

Atopic Dermatitis

A Chronic Inflammatory Skin Condition, Commonly Known As Eczema, Causing Itchy, Red, And Inflamed Skin. It's Often Linked To Allergies Or Asthma And Tends To Flare Up During Specific Triggers.

Eczema

A General Term For Skin Conditions That Cause The Skin To Become Red, Itchy, And Inflamed. The Most Common Form Is Atopic Dermatitis, But There Are Other Types Like Contact Dermatitis

Cellulitis

Cellulitis Is A Bacterial Skin Infection That Affects The Deeper Layers Of The Skin And The Underlying Tissues. It Occurs When. Bacteria (Commonly Streptococcus Or Staphylococcus) Enter The Skin Through A Break, Cut, Or Wound, Leading To Inflammation And Infection.

Impetigo

Impetigo Is A Highly Contagious Bacterial Skin Infection That Primarily Affects Infants And Young Children, Although It Can Occur In People Of All Ages. It Is Usually Caused By Two Types Of Bacteria: Streptococcus Pyogenes (Group A Strep) And Staphylococcus Aureus (Staph).

Exanthems and drug eruptions

Exanthems Are Rashes Caused By Viral Infections, Such As Measles Or Chickenpox. Drug Eruptions Are Skin Reactions Triggered By Medications, Varying From Mild Rashes To Severe Reactions. Both Require Proper Diagnosis And Management
